Hospital and Lab Partnership

  • INCREASE HEALTH SYSTEM REVENUES
  • REDUCE INPATIENT LABORATORY TESTING COSTS
  • REDUCE DOWNSTREAM HEALTHCARE COSTS

Most hospital laboratories maintain significant unused capacity, resulting in higher testing costs and under-leveraged capital investments. Additionally many health systems are also experiencing laboratory cost increases of 2% to 4% annually.

Sonic Reference Laboratory is partnering with health systems who are interested in any, or all of the following:

  • Leveraging laboratory data to reduce downstream healthcare costs
  • Provide a stable, predictable, frequently lower inpatient laboratory cost structure
  • Eliminating capital investments in the laboratory
  • Enhancing health system profits through expanded outreach laboratory services
  • Improving integration of inpatient and outreach laboratory services, including electronic health records (EHR) integration
  • Providing access to Sonic's global supply and lab equipment cost structure
  • Monetizing all or a portion of outreach laboratory business to allow redeployment of capital to strategically important health system service lines
