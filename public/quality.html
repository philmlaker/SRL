Quality


With a quality program based on the Quality System Essentials defined by the Clinical and Laboratory Standards Institute (CLSI), SRL aims for best practice by elevating the commitment to quality from compliance with minimum requirements to the highest achievable level.


Sonic Reference Laboratory Quality Policy

Sonic Reference Laboratory is committed to providing quality laboratory services, continually striving for excellence in the following areas:

  • Patient safety, including accurate and timely test results
  • Employee health and safety
  • Exceeding customer expectations
  • Innovative and customized laboratory services
  • Collaborative, continuous education
  • Compliance with regulator, accrediation and ISO 15189 standards
