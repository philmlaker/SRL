Featured Testing

BRCA

SRL is proud to offer BRCA testing utilizing Next Generation Sequencing Technology with a validated 99.98% Sensitivity and 99.98% Specificity. Testing for mutation in BRCA1 and BRCA2 is recommended when a person's individual or family history suggest the possible presence of a harmful mutation.

Epi Procolon

The first and only FDA-approved blood test for colorectal cancer screening. Epi proColon is a routine blood test for those average-risk persons, age 50 and older, who are unqilling or unable to be screen by other methods. Add Epi proColon to your tool kit for those patients who avoid CRC screening.

Harmony NIPT

Harmony is validated for pregnant women of all ages* in the general population and can be performed as early as 10 weeks. Uses a proprietary, target DNA-based technology that analyzes fragment from the specific chromosome of interest, rathar than all chromosome.

Lipoprint

Lipoprotein Subfractions Testing System is the only FDA-cleared diagnostic test that analyzes all lipoprotein fractions and subfracts in fasting serum or plasma. It also is the only method that identifies and measures cholesterol of all atherogenic and non-atherogenic LDL and HDL subfractions.

Respiratory Pathogen Panel

The RPP test offered by Sonic Reference Laboratory is intended for detection and identification of viral and bacterial agents in nasopharyngeal swab collected from symptomatic adult and pediatric patients suspected of respiratory tract infections.
